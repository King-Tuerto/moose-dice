Game Rules

GoalFirst player to reach 10,000 pts wins (after final round)
OpeningNeed 500 pts in a single turn to get on the board
Banking minimum350 pts once you're on the board
Hot DiceIf all 6 dice score, keep the points and roll all 6 again
BustRoll with no scoring dice → lose all points for that turn
Keeping diceYou must keep at least 1 scoring die per roll. Once a scoring die is kept, you may also hold non-scoring dice (e.g. keep a pair hoping to complete a triple)
LockingKept dice become locked on your next roll — you can't pick them back up
Final roundOnce someone hits 10,000, every other player gets exactly one more turn
